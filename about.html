About RDL

Who We Are

We are New Shivkrupa Automobiles, manufacturers of high-quality two-wheeler ABS fiber body partsunder the brand RDL Genuine Parts.Founded by Mr. Dinesh B. Londhe, a skilled mechanic turned entrepreneur, the company began its journey from a small workshop in Kurduwadiwhich still operates today.

Now an ISO 9001:2015 certified organization,we are committed to maintaining quality, trust, and innovation in every product. With complete in-house facilities, we ensure consistent performance and durability from moulding to final packing.

We also produce our own corrugated boxes and LD bags, and operate a self-owned logistics system that enables fast and reliable delivery across Maharashtra, Karnataka, Telangana, and all over India.Backed by years of experience and strong family values, Team RDL continues to deliver precisely finished and mechanic-friendly products trusted nationwide.

THE FOUNDER : MR.DINESH LONDHE

The story of New Shivkrupa Automobiles begins with Mr. Dinesh B. Londhe, a passionate mechanic from Kurduwadi, who started a small two-wheeler garage in 1999. His dedication, skill, and honesty soon earned him the trust of customers and mechanics across the region. With a vision to deliver the same workshop-level precision on a larger scale, he founded the RDL brand in 2013, beginning as a trader of two-wheeler fiber parts. Over time, his commitment to quality transformed RDL into a respected manufacturing brand trusted across India.

Now at 46 years of age, Mr. Londhe continues to guide the company with his deep technical knowledge and values of quality, discipline, and innovation.
